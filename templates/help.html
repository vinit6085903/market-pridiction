Frequently Asked Questions
How does AlphaPredict AI generate market predictions?

AlphaPredict AI uses advanced machine learning algorithms that analyze historical market data, technical indicators, sentiment analysis from news and social media, and macroeconomic factors. Our AI models are trained on millions of data points and continuously updated to adapt to changing market conditions. The system provides predictions with confidence levels to help you make informed trading decisions.

What is the accuracy rate of predictions?

Our AI prediction accuracy varies by market and timeframe. On average, we maintain an accuracy rate of 85-92% for short-term predictions (1-24 hours) and 75-85% for medium-term predictions (1-7 days). Accuracy is higher for high-liquidity markets like major forex pairs and cryptocurrencies. You can view the current accuracy rate on your dashboard.

Note: All predictions are probabilistic in nature and should be used as one of many factors in your trading decisions. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

How do I execute trades based on predictions?

AlphaPredict AI provides trading signals but does not execute trades automatically. To execute a trade:

  1. Review the prediction details including confidence level, target price, and stop loss
  2. Click the "Execute Trade" button on any prediction card
  3. Configure your trade parameters (position size, leverage if applicable)
  4. Confirm the trade in your connected brokerage account

We recommend using proper risk management and not risking more than 1-2% of your capital on any single trade.

Can I customize which markets I receive predictions for?

Yes, you can fully customize your prediction feed. Go to Settings > Market Preferences to select which asset classes and specific instruments you want to monitor. You can choose from:

  • Cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, and 50+ others)
  • Forex pairs (Major, minor, and exotic pairs)
  • Commodities (Gold, Silver, Oil, etc.)
  • Stock indices (S&P 500, NASDAQ, etc.)

You can also set notification preferences for each market type.

How do I connect my trading account?

AlphaPredict AI currently supports API connections to major exchanges and brokers including Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Interactive Brokers, and OANDA. To connect your account:

  1. Go to Settings > Account Connections
  2. Select your broker/exchange from the list
  3. Generate API keys in your trading account (read-only for security)
  4. Enter the API key and secret in AlphaPredict AI
  5. Test the connection to verify it's working

Security Tip: Always use read-only API keys to prevent unauthorized trading actions.
